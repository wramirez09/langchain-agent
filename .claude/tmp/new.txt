Treatment: Percutaneous Image-Guided Lumbar Decompression for Lumbar Spinal Stenosis (PILD)
CPT: Not specified in Medicare NCD (verify coding with your MAC)
ICD-10: Not provided
Diagnosis: Lumbar spinal stenosis (LSS)
Medical History: Not provided

Prior Authorization Required: CONDITIONAL

Medicare Fee-For-Service covers PILD only when furnished within a CMS-approved Coverage with Evidence Development (CED) clinical study.
While traditional “prior authorization” is generally not required by Medicare FFS for this service, coverage is conditional on documented enrollment in an approved study and strict adherence to the study protocol. Medicare Advantage plans may have their own prior authorization processes.
Medical Necessity Criteria:


Service must be performed for Medicare beneficiaries with LSS enrolled in a CMS-approved CED clinical study under SSA §1862(a)(1)(E).

The approved study must address clinically meaningful improvement in:

Function and/or quality of life

Pain reduction

Overall clinical management of LSS compared to other treatments

The study protocol must include:

A specified statistical analysis plan

A defined minimum length of patient follow-up

CMS approval of the protocol prior to billing Medicare
