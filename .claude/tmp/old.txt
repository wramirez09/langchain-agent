Medicare Fee-for-Service coverage is conditional under a national policy and limited to beneficiaries enrolled in a CMS-approved clinical study (Coverage with Evidence Development). Outside of this setting, the service is non-covered.
Note: Traditional Medicare generally does not operate a separate prior authorization program for this service; however, Medicare Advantage plans commonly require plan-level prior authorization even when an NCD exists.
Medical Necessity Criteria Coverage is available only under Coverage with Evidence Development (CED) when ALL are met:


The procedure is performed as part of a clinical study approved under section 1862(a)(1)(E) of the Social Security Act.

The clinical study meets CMS standards, including:

Prospective, randomized, controlled design

Validated outcome measures (e.g., function, quality of life, pain)

Clinically appropriate comparator treatment

Evaluation of how beneficiary characteristics affect outcomes and duration of benefit

The study is registered on ClinicalTrials.gov and conforms to CMS standards of scientific integrity.
