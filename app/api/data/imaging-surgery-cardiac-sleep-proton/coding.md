\## Scope

\- Radiation oncology coding/utilization standards for:

\- Dosimetry planning

\- Image guidance

\- Physician treatment management

\- Physics

\- Simulations

\- Treatment devices

\- ICD-10 codes:

\- None listed in the document.

\- CPT/HCPCS codes referenced:

\- 77014, 77280, 77285, 77290, +77293, 77295

\- 77300, 77301, 77306, 77307, 77316, 77317, 77318, 77321, 77331, 77332,
77333, 77334, 77336, 77338, 77370, 77387, 77399

\- 77417, 77427, 77431, 77432, 77435, 77469, 77470, 77499

\- 77790

\- G6001, G6002, G6017

\## Medical Necessity Criteria

\### Dosimetry Planning

\#### 3-Dimensional Radiotherapy Plan

\- Relevant code:

\- 77295

\- Criteria:

\- One plan may be approved per course of 3D external beam treatment.

\- One plan may be approved per course of SRS or SBRT.

\- May be appropriate for HDR/LDR brachytherapy, proton therapy, neutron
beam therapy, and SIRT if 3D criteria are met.

\- An additional plan may be appropriate only if:

\- Medical necessity is documented, and

\- A second CT or MRI dataset is acquired, and

\- The new dataset demonstrates a meaningful change in anatomy and/or
tumor volume.

\- Required documentation:

\- New CT or MRI dataset when requesting an additional plan.

\- Documentation of meaningful change in anatomy/tumor volume.

\- Documentation of medical necessity.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Includes isodose planning work.

\- Do not bill with 77306, 77307, or 77321 for the same treatment
segment/volume.

\- Not billable for IMRT boost plans.

\- Only 1 unit per course unless re-planning criteria are met.

\#### Basic Radiation Dosimetry Calculation

\- Relevant code:

\- 77300

\- Criteria:

\- Approvable for 2D treatment when hand calculations are performed.

\- For 3D and IMRT, billable as 1 per medically necessary
field/port/angle/arc.

\- May be approved for stereotactic therapy based on submitted number of
fields/portals/angles/arcs.

\- Up to 2 units may be approved for SIRT.

\- Required documentation:

\- Physician-prescribed treatment.

\- Submitted number of fields/ports/angles/arcs for stereotactic
treatment.

\- Additional supporting documentation and/or physician review if
quantity exceeds 10.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Not separately billable with 77306, 77307, or 77321.

\- Bundled into 77316-77318.

\- Bundled into HDR delivery codes 77767, 77768, 77770-77772.

\- Multiple calculations per beam angle are not allowed.

\- IMRT planning system calculations are included in 77301 and not
separately billable.

\#### IMRT Plan

\- Relevant code:

\- 77301

\- Criteria:

\- One IMRT plan may be approved per course of medically necessary IMRT
treatment.

\- Additional IMRT plans require:

\- A new CT or MRI scan for planning, and

\- A physician medical necessity statement, and

\- Meaningful change in target volumes and/or patient anatomy.

\- Required documentation:

\- New CT/MRI dataset.

\- Physician statement supporting medical necessity.

\- Evidence that the new dataset changes planning volumes.

\- Dose constraints for organs at risk and dose goals for the target.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- A traditional field-in-field/forward-planned hotspot technique is not
considered IMRT.

\- If no new CT/MRI dataset is obtained, an additional IMRT plan is not
billable.

\- Associated standards for treatment devices, calculations, and edits
still apply.

\#### Teletherapy Isodose Plans

\- Relevant codes:

\- 77306, 77307

\- Criteria:

\- One isodose plan allowed per volume of interest.

\- One plan may be approved for 2D external beam and 3D external beam
boost when a 3D plan has already been billed in the same course.

\- May be separately billable if the isodose-plan volume is separate and
distinct from a 3D-plan volume.

\- Required documentation:

\- Isodose plan reflecting dose distribution and anatomy.

\- Documentation showing separate/distinct treatment volume if billed
with another plan.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Contiguous volumes are treated as one volume of interest.

\- Additional isodose plans are not allowed for contiguous fields.

\- Not billable on the same date of service as 77301 for the same
volume.

\#### Brachytherapy Isodose Plans

\- Relevant codes:

\- 77316, 77317, 77318

\- Criteria:

\- Only one plan per unique treatment volume/procedure.

\- Additional plans in the same course may be approved if each
application requires a new image set and new plan.

\- Up to 2 plans may be approved for prostate seed implants.

\- A 3D plan may be substituted if a 3D plan has not already been
authorized in the same course.

\- Required documentation:

\- Plan identifying internal devices/applicators.

\- Source placement locations.

\- Doses to regions of interest.

\- If requesting an additional plan:

\- New image set

\- Physician rationale explaining why the prior plan cannot be reused

\- Medical necessity for the new plan

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- If the same plan is reused for each fraction, no additional plan is
billable.

\- Additional billing requires a medically necessary change such as new
placement or anatomy change.

\#### Special Teletherapy Port Plan

\- Relevant code:

\- 77321

\- Criteria:

\- May be approved per volume of interest.

\- May be approved for all computer-based electron treatment planning.

\- May be approved for proton and neutron beam isodose planning.

\- May be approved for point-dose calculations for total body
irradiation special particle planning.

\- Required documentation:

\- Isodose plan for electron/special particle planning.

\- For hemi-body/total-body planning, separate supporting documentation
if no isodose plan is required.

\- Documentation that a separate volume of interest exists if billed
with another dosimetry plan.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Not billable simply because electrons are used.

\- Not billable with 77301, 77295, 77306, or 77307 on the same date for
the same volume.

\- 77295 may be used instead for proton/neutron planning.

\#### Special Dosimetry

\- Relevant code:

\- 77331

\- Criteria:

\- Billable once per port/field/angle per course for 2D, electron, or 3D
external beam treatment.

\- Must be medically necessary and ordered by a radiation oncologist.

\- Diode requests may be approved in a quantity equal to the number of
ports/fields/arcs/angles.

\- Other radiation modalities require case-specific rationale and
physician review.

\- Required documentation:

\- Radiation oncologist order.

\- Medical necessity rationale.

\- Case-specific rationale for modalities outside 2D/electron/3D.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Not routine.

\- Not billable for QA or output measurements with brachytherapy, IMRT,
IORT, proton therapy, SRS, or SBRT.

\#### Unlisted Dosimetry Procedure

\- Relevant code:

\- 77399

\- Criteria:

\- May be approved only when no existing code accurately describes the
service.

\- Service must not be bundled or packaged into another code.

\- Required documentation:

\- Formal request.

\- Rationale.

\- Documentation of the service.

\- Physician review of rationale/documentation.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Not medically necessary when another established code exists.

\- Not medically necessary for CT-MRI fusion with IMRT planning.

\### Image Guidance

\#### CT Guidance for Placement of Radiation Therapy Fields

\- Relevant code:

\- 77014

\- Criteria:

\- May be used when CT imaging for planning is performed separately from
simulation.

\- May occur at another facility, another place of service, or later in
treatment for re-planning/reduction of volumes.

\- Billable if:

\- Target/tumor localization data are acquired immediately before
treatment,

\- Data are fused and registered in the same treatment position,

\- Data/shifts are reviewed,

\- Repositioning is performed if needed and verified.

\- Required documentation:

\- CT acquisition separate from simulation.

\- Fusion/registration details.

\- Review by radiation oncologist/medical physicist/trained therapist
under supervision.

\- Patient repositioning/offset verification if performed.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Bundled when performed with the initial simulation on the same day.

\#### Image-Guided Radiation Therapy

\- Relevant code:

\- 77387

\- Criteria:

\- One IGRT procedure allowed per external beam fraction.

\- Can be used regardless of imaging type.

\- Can be approved for 2D/3D, IMRT, and proton therapy.

\- Required documentation:

\- Appropriate documentation for each occurrence.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Not medically necessary for superficial radiation therapy.

\- IGRT, port images, and simulations are not billable on the same date
for the same treatment site.

\- Technical component is bundled when performed on the same date as
IMRT delivery (77385, 77386).

\#### Port Images

\- Relevant code:

\- 77417

\- Criteria:

\- Billable once per 5 fractions of therapy.

\- Quantity is 1 per 5 fractions regardless of the number of images
obtained.

\- Required documentation:

\- Physician order.

\- At least one image taken.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Not billable for brachytherapy, electron therapy, IORT, SBRT, or SRS.

\- Not billable on the same date as 77280 or 77387 for the same
treatment site.

\#### Ultrasound IGRT

\- Relevant code:

\- G6001

\- Criteria:

\- Used with 2D and 3D treatment.

\- May be billed when trained personnel perform ultrasound localization
under physician supervision in the office setting.

\- Required documentation:

\- Performance by trained personnel.

\- Physician supervision.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Technical component is bundled in the hospital setting.

\- Not medically necessary for superficial radiation treatment of skin
cancers or keloids.

\#### Stereoscopic X-Ray IGRT

\- Relevant code:

\- G6002

\- Criteria:

\- Used immediately before 3D or IMRT delivery.

\- Images may be acquired with or without fiducial markers.

\- Images must be fused/registered and corrected shifts confirmed.

\- Required documentation:

\- Orthogonal imaging.

\- Registration with pretreatment radiographs.

\- Calculated and confirmed shifts.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- None specifically stated beyond standard imaging/localization
requirements.

\#### Intra-Fraction IGRT

\- Relevant code:

\- G6017

\- Criteria:

\- Used for intra-fraction localization/tracking during each fraction.

\- Used with implanted transponders or 3D positional/surface tracking.

\- Required documentation:

\- Tracking/localization performed during treatment fraction.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- None specifically stated in retrieved text.

\### Physician Treatment Management

\#### External Beam Treatment Management

\- Relevant codes:

\- 77427, 77431

\- Criteria:

\- 77427:

\- One per 5 fractions of external beam treatment.

\- Also allowed when 3 or 4 fractions remain at course end.

\- At least one face-to-face visit is required during each 5-fraction
span.

\- 77431:

\- One time for a complete non-stereotactic external beam course of 1 or
2 fractions.

\- At least one face-to-face visit is required.

\- Required documentation:

\- Face-to-face physician encounter.

\- Management elements:

\- Clinical evaluation/management

\- Review of on-treatment images if any

\- Review of dosimetry/dose delivery/treatment parameters

\- Review of patient setup

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- 77427/77431 should not be billed for stereotactic therapy.

\- 77427/77431 are not billable for brachytherapy courses.

\#### Stereotactic Treatment Management

\- Relevant codes:

\- 77432, 77435

\- Criteria:

\- Maximum stereotactic deliveries: 5.

\- 77432:

\- One cranial stereotactic treatment session.

\- 77435:

\- One extracranial SBRT fraction, including spine, or

\- Two to five intracranial fractionated stereotactic fractions.

\- Required documentation:

\- Provider-authorized stereotactic treatment course/fractions.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Courses with more than 5 stereotactic treatments are not authorized
as stereotactic management.

\#### Intraoperative Radiation Treatment Management

\- Relevant code:

\- 77469

\- Criteria:

\- Maximum quantity is 1 per course.

\- Required documentation:

\- Intraoperative treatment management service.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Does not include evaluation and management outside the intraoperative
session.

\#### Special Treatment Procedure

\- Relevant code:

\- 77470

\- Criteria:

\- Must be requested by the provider.

\- Maximum quantity is 1 per course.

\- Allowed for:

\- Pediatric patient requiring daily anesthesia and daily physician
personal supervision

\- BID treatment

\- Brachytherapy, including SIRT

\- Combined external beam radiation and brachytherapy

\- Concurrent cytotoxic chemotherapy, targeted therapy, or immunotherapy
with external beam radiation

\- Hyperthermia

\- Difficult patient setup

\- Oral radiation treatment, including radioactive iodine-131 for
hyperthyroidism and selected well-differentiated thyroid carcinoma

\- Radioimmunotherapy with external beam treatment

\- Reconstruction of prior plans, complex planning, and physics input

\- Total body irradiation or hemi-body radiation

\- Must also meet all of the following:

\- Rationale supplied by the radiation oncologist

\- Not previously preauthorized in the same course

\- Rationale explains additional time/effort beyond routine
planning/treatment

\- Required documentation:

\- Provider request

\- Radiation oncologist rationale

\- Supporting documentation of extra planning/management/treatment time

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Not allowed for routine use of brachytherapy, 3D treatment, IMRT,
SBRT, or SRS without extra documented complexity.

\- Not allowed for routine contouring/fusion for 3D/IMRT planning.

\- Not allowed for review of a multi-phase plan when a composite plan is
created.

\- Not allowed for DIBH.

\- Not allowed for hormonal therapy with concurrent external beam
radiation.

\- Not allowed for comorbidities unless they complicate radiation
delivery.

\- Sequential chemo/targeted/immunotherapy is not reportable unless
prior therapy affects current planning/dose.

\#### Unlisted Therapeutic Radiology Treatment Management

\- Relevant code:

\- 77499

\- Criteria:

\- Used only when another established treatment management code does not
accurately describe the service.

\- Required documentation:

\- Service must be distinct from established management codes.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Should not be used when 77427, 77431, 77432, 77435, 77469, or 77470
accurately describes the service.

\### Physics

\#### Continuing Medical Physics

\- Relevant code:

\- 77336

\- Criteria:

\- Billable once per 5 fractions of treatment.

\- One additional unit may be billed when 3-4 fractions remain at the
end of treatment.

\- Billable one time for courses consisting of 1-2 fractions.

\- Required documentation:

\- Physics review/chart check within each applicable 5-fraction period.

\- Review of treatment parameters, QA of dose delivery, and patient
treatment documentation.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Not billable if review occurs well after treatment is finalized.

\- Not billable for treatment involving 77401.

\- Not billable for single-fraction brachytherapy.

\#### Special Physics Consultation

\- Relevant code:

\- 77370

\- Criteria:

\- Allowed once per course.

\- Must be requested by the provider.

\- Appropriate for selected non-routine scenarios requiring medical
physicist expertise, including:

\- Prior radiation with cumulative dose assessment

\- Analysis of customized beam modification/special blocking to protect
critical organs

\- Complex electron/photon port relationships

\- Fetal dose calculation

\- Coronary artery brachytherapy

\- 3D image fusion by a qualified medical physicist for 3D planning

\- Corrective measures for discrepancies/errors

\- Physicist presence during exceptional complex fraction delivery

\- Implanted cardiac device safety review

\- SIRT

\- Special brachytherapy equipment developed for an individual patient

\- Must also meet all of the following:

\- Provider rationale supplied

\- Not previously authorized in the same course

\- Rationale explains why physicist expertise is needed and why another
code does not describe the work

\- Required documentation:

\- Specific physician request

\- Work performed by the medical physicist

\- Physician acknowledgement of results

\- Patient-specific rationale when scenario is not specifically listed

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Not for treatment-planning summaries.

\- Not for IMRT QA as part of routine IMRT planning.

\- Not approvable for:

\- DIBH

\- Electron cutout measurements

\- In-vivo dosimetry

\- Routine verification of dose distribution

\- Secondary monitor unit calculations

\- Services defined by another CPT code

\- SRS/SBRT with IMRT planning

\- Treatment planning summaries

\- Requests outside criteria require peer-to-peer review.

\#### Supervision and Handling of Radiation Source

\- Relevant code:

\- 77790

\- Criteria:

\- Allowed once per LDR brachytherapy or therapeutic radiopharmaceutical
application.

\- Required documentation:

\- Handling/loading service for radioactive source application.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Bundled with certain treatment delivery codes, including 77750,
77761-77763, 77778, 77789, and 79101-79445.

\### Simulations

\#### Simulation Services

\- Relevant codes:

\- 77280, 77285, 77290, +77293

\- Criteria:

\- 77280: simple simulation of a single treatment area.

\- 77285: intermediate simulation of two separate treatment areas.

\- 77290: complex simulation of three or more treatment areas, or any
number of areas if particle therapy, rotation/arc therapy, complex
blocking, custom shielding blocks, brachytherapy, hyperthermia probe
verification, or contrast is involved.

\- One initial simulation may be approved per modality except IMRT
courses.

\- Verification simulation is billed as 77280 only.

\- For 2D/3D treatment, one verification simulation may be approved per
phase when IGRT is not authorized.

\- For 3D treatment, 77280 may be approved in addition to IGRT if
specifically requested and clinically justified.

\- For HDR brachytherapy, one verification simulation may be approved
per treatment.

\- Required documentation:

\- Treatment position setup.

\- Images/data needed to develop a plan.

\- Port blocking/isocenter/patient-position verification for
verification simulations.

\- All ports imaged, reviewed, and approved before treatment start.

\- Clinical rationale if requested with IGRT for 3D.

\- Work performed and documented if more than one simulation is allowed
in brachytherapy.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Only one simulation charge per date of service except specific
brachytherapy exceptions.

\- Simulations for IMRT planning are bundled into 77301.

\- Verification simulations are not billable with IMRT courses.

\- Only 1 verification simulation per phase.

\- Simple simulation should not be reported daily for routine placement
of treatment fields.

\### Treatment Devices

\#### Simple / Intermediate / Complex Treatment Devices

\- Relevant codes:

\- 77332, 77333, 77334

\- Criteria:

\- Immobilization devices are billable as many times as they are
created.

\- One complex immobilization device may be approved per external beam
course if created during simulation.

\- For brachytherapy:

\- One treatment device may be approved for each placement when device
changes and medical necessity supports the change.

\- Same device/size/type used each fraction is billable only once at
initial placement.

\- A device left in place for more than one fraction is billed once.

\- For prostate external beam therapy:

\- One complex device may be approved for a custom-filled rectal
balloon.

\- One simple device may be approved for a standard-fill rectal balloon.

\- Beam-modifying devices may be approved up to documented maximums per
phase:

\- 2D/3D/IMRT: 10

\- Proton therapy: 10

\- SBRT: 10

\- LDR brachytherapy: 1

\- HDR brachytherapy: 1

\- SIRT: 2

\- Required documentation:

\- Device creation by the provider.

\- Documentation supporting each beam-modifying device.

\- Medical necessity when a brachytherapy device changes.

\- Documentation of custom fill for rectal balloon if billed as complex.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- If an immobilization device is not created by the provider, it should
not be billed.

\- If a rectal balloon is not used, it should not be requested.

\- Only one beam-modifying device is billable per port/field.

\- Mirrored parallel-opposed pair counts as quantity 1.

\- Additional services may require review for individual medical
necessity.

\- 77334 is not billable for MLC-based IMRT devices.

\#### IMRT Planning/Treatment Device

\- Relevant code:

\- 77338

\- Criteria:

\- One unit only per IMRT plan creation.

\- One IMRT treatment device may be approved per phase of medically
necessary MLC-based IMRT planning/treatment.

\- Includes IMRT treatment and IMRT planning used with proton therapy,
SRS, and SBRT.

\- May be billed for an IMRT boost even if an additional IMRT plan is
not allowed.

\- Required documentation:

\- MLC-based IMRT plan creation.

\- Limitations and exclusions:

\- Only billable with IMRT planning.

\- Not billable with other types of isodose planning.

\- Reserved for MLC-based IMRT devices only.

\- Compensator-based IMRT devices use 77334 instead.

\## Required Documentation Summary

\- New CT/MRI dataset for re-planning when required.

\- Physician medical necessity statement when requesting additional
plans.

\- Provider/radiation oncologist request for special
procedures/consultations.

\- Clinical rationale for:

\- Additional plan requests

\- Special dosimetry outside standard indications

\- Verification simulation with IGRT for 3D treatment

\- Special treatment procedure

\- Special physics consultation

\- Unlisted procedures

\- Isodose plan when required.

\- Separate documentation for total-body/hemi-body planning when an
isodose plan is not required.

\- Image fusion/registration and shift verification documentation for
image guidance.

\- Physics review/chart-check documentation within the applicable
treatment period.

\- Documentation that devices were created by the provider and are
medically necessary.

\- Physician acknowledgement of special physics consultation results.

\## Global Limitations and Exclusions

\- No ICD-10 diagnosis list is provided.

\- Many services are bundled and therefore not separately billable in
specific scenarios.

\- Several services are limited to:

\- One per course

\- One per phase

\- One per volume of interest

\- One per treatment fraction

\- One per five fractions

\- Same-day billing conflicts apply across simulation, IGRT, port
imaging, IMRT planning, and dosimetry services.
